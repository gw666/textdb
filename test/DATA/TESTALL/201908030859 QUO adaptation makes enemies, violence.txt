Adaptation is provocation. Adaptation is incitement to violence.
----

in "The Things", by Peter Watts, in _Clarkesworld: Year Four_ anthology.

The speaker is the alien from the movie _The Thing_, but told from the alien's POV. It is its explanation why the humans were so hostile.

However, this idea is relevant to human behavior. It is consistent with people's behavior when they are aggressive towards others who are behaving differently, with any situation where one group does something adaptive and the other violently opposes it.

Examples: global warming; (maybe) civil rights movement; in religions, *some* things that "hardliners" and conservatives are opposed to (e.g., letting women be ministers)
